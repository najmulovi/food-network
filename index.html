Dal Makhani

Dal Makhani is the tastiest Dal of the India and is also famous all over the world. Dal Makhani is made up by using mixture of kidney Beans and Udad daal. But the taste in this dal is brought up by adding cream in it which is a milk product. If you do not have Cream then you can also add Malai of Milk.

Chilli Chicken

Indo-Chinese and just divine, skip the take-out and savour this boneless chilli chicken in the comfort of your home. Team it with fried rice or just have it stand-alone, this spicy and delectable dish is a show-stealer.

Chicken Soup

Thai Chicken Soup (Tom Ka Gai) has that distinctive Thai flavor—a balance of spicy, salty, sweet and sour.Tom Ka Gai can either be served as an appetizer or as the star player (noodles can be added if you're planning to make this soup as the main entree).
